## Design Constraints

All core glyphs must:

- Represent a **state, force, or relationship** (not an object)
- Be interpretable by **humans and AI agents**
- Occupy a **region** in semantic grids, not a single fixed meaning
- Support metaphorical, emotional, ritual, and computational readings

No glyph in this set is allowed to:

- Assert truth
- Enforce action
- Override consent

## Canon Rules for Expansion

New glyph proposals must:

- Fill a _structural gap_ (not a synonym)
- Declare what they are **not**
